Killer Weakness

Current Classification System is a Closed System


  1. deciding how to classify the one copy of a multidisciplnary/multitopic book is a bad trip.
  2. not suitable for any other printed materials such as tracts, pamphlets, magazines, newspapers, etc.
  3. original documents such as the John Jay papers must be given their own library, complete with custom classification of the docs.
  4. treats multimedia as if it doesn't exist.
  5. has no flexibility, built-in or otherwise, to include technologies/formats not invented as yet.
