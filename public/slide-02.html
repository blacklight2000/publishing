Physical Books Are High Maintenance

Issues With Physical Books


  1. They have legs - they get misplaced, stolen, unreturned, and plain lost
  2. They get disfigured - pages written on, pages ripped, pages worm eaten, etc.
  3. They get old and die - if written on paper thgat is not acid free degrades within 30 years
  4. They require life support - a tightly temperature, humidity, lighting controlled environment
  5. They are not ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) compliant, at least for those with visual disabilities
